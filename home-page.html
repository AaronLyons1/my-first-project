diagnosing

Diagnosing the problems in your car is an amazing start to
being able to fix whats wrong or helping the mechanic if you
know what is going wrong or where the fault is.

  1. When driving, listen to where the noise is coming from
  2. Decide on what noise it is (is it a "growl" noise or a "whine")
    deciding on what noise is very useful in understanding what is going wrong.
  3. Figure out what could ber causing your noise, is it when you speed
    up it gets louder or is it when go on different road surfaces the noise
    is a different tone
  4. Once all this is decided you can give the information to your mechanic, it will be great help!
