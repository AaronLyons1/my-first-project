How to change a tyre

  1. Locate the jack, usually stored in boot of car
  2. locate tool kit also found in the boot of the car
  3. Before jacking the car up, you need to crack the
    wheel nuts. To do this place the on a tyre nut roughly
    90Â° to the ground and push anti-clockwise.
  4. when all the nuts make a *crack* noice, jack the car into the air.
  5. Find jack points on car, look underneath car usually flat spot underneath
    the car at the doors.
  6. Once car in the air, fully take off wheel nuts followed by the wheel.
    (note: wheel might need to be pulled off, be careful not to pull car off the jack. )

How to put on a spare tyre

  1. Complete steps above before starting here
  2. Find spare tyre, usually underneath boot cover or underneath back of car
  3. Place wheel on wheel hub and line up the holes for the bolts
  4. When all the bolts are in the holes and slightly tightened lower car
  5. When car is on the ground further tighten the bolts until u cant anymore
  6. Go straight to a garage and get a new tyre for your wheel.
